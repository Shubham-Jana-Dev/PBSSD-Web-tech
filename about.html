Our History

The history of this project began as a simple classroom exercise designed to teach the fundamentals of HTML and web architecture. Over the past few weeks, it has grown into a comprehensive display of technical skills, including asset management and internal navigation. This journey has taught me the importance of perseverance and attention to detail when building digital experiences for a global audience.
